Support requests are reviewed as soon as reasonably possible.

What to include

To help investigate an issue, please include:

  • Your device model and iOS version.
  • The app screen or feature where the issue happened.
  • A short description of what you expected and what happened.
  • Any relevant screenshots, if available.
  • The email address used to sign in, if the issue is account-related.

Common support topics

  • Ticket alert setup, expiry, cancellation, and push notifications.
  • Race availability, restock, low-stock, and sold-out information.
  • HYRESULT import and saved race analysis.
  • Account access and notification permissions.
  • Bug reports and feature feedback.

Independent app

HYROX Lab is an independent companion app and is not the official HYROX app.
