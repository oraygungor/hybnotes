What to include

To help investigate an issue quickly, please include:

  • Your device model and iOS version.
  • The app version, if visible in the app or App Store build.
  • The screen or feature where the issue happened.
  • A short description of what you expected and what happened.
  • Any relevant screenshots or screen recordings, if available.
  • The email address used to sign in, if the issue is account-related.
  • The event name, race category, or HYRESULT link/code if the issue is race or ticket related.

Account and data requests

HYROX Lab supports account-related requests including sign-in help, profile questions, notification settings, and account deletion requests.

If your request is related to an account, include the email address used to sign in. If you used Sign in with Apple and chose a private relay email, include that relay address if it is available to you.

For privacy details, see the HYROX Lab Privacy Policy.

Notifications

HYROX Lab may send push notifications for ticket alerts, restocks, low-stock changes, sold-out changes, new event availability, alert expiry, and relevant app updates.

If notifications are not arriving, check iOS notification permissions first, then contact support with your device model, iOS version, app version, and the event or alert involved.

Accuracy and availability

HYROX Lab aims to show useful race, event, ticket, and availability information, but availability can change quickly and may differ from the official purchase or event source.

For final ticket purchases, race registration, event rules, or official event decisions, always rely on the official HYROX or ticketing source.

Independent app

HYROX Lab is an independent companion app and is not the official HYROX app.

HYROX names, events, and related references are used to describe app functionality and race-related information.
