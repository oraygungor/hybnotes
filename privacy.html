1. Who we are

HYROX Lab is an independent companion app for tracking HYROX race availability, ticket alerts, event discovery, and race performance analysis.

HYROX Lab is not the official HYROX app.

Contact: oray.gungor@gmail.com

2. Information we collect

Depending on how you use the app, HYROX Lab may process the following information:

  • Account information: email address, display name, profile photo, sign-in provider, user ID, account tier, creation date, update date, and last login time.
  • Ticket alert information: selected event, selected race category, alert type, alert status, stock state, creation time, expiry time, trigger time, and notification state.
  • Saved race information: HYRESULT code or link, event name, location, race date, division, race type, finish time, and split or performance information used for analysis.
  • Notification information: device push token, platform, device information, notification enabled state, and timestamps related to notification registration.
  • Admin and operational logs: limited records used to manage support, notifications, alert rules, saved races, and app reliability.
  • Technical information: app version, platform, error states, and diagnostic information needed to operate and improve the app.

3. How we use information

HYROX Lab uses information to:

  • Provide account access and authentication.
  • Show HYROX event and ticket availability information.
  • Create, manage, trigger, expire, and cancel ticket alerts.
  • Send push notifications related to ticket alerts, new races, app messages, and relevant account or app events.
  • Load, save, and analyze race results.
  • Operate admin support tools and prevent misuse.
  • Maintain app security, reliability, and performance.

4. Sign-in and third-party services

HYROX Lab uses Firebase services for authentication, database storage, cloud functions, and push-notification related operations.

If you sign in with Google or Apple, those providers may process information according to their own terms and privacy policies.

Push notifications may be sent through platform notification services and Expo or Firebase-related infrastructure. You can control notification permissions from your device settings.

5. Data sharing

HYROX Lab does not sell your personal information.

Information may be processed by service providers necessary to operate the app, such as authentication, database, cloud hosting, cloud functions, diagnostics where used, and push notification infrastructure.

Information may also be disclosed if required by law, to protect the app, or to prevent misuse.

6. Notifications

HYROX Lab may send notifications related to ticket alerts, alert expiry, new race availability, admin messages, and app-related updates.

You can disable app notifications through your device settings. Some notification preferences may also be controlled inside the app when available.

7. Data retention

HYROX Lab keeps account, alert, saved race, notification, and operational information for as long as needed to provide the app, support users, maintain security, and meet operational needs.

You may request account-related support or deletion by contacting the support email below.

8. Your choices

  • You can manage notification permissions in iOS settings.
  • You can cancel ticket alerts inside the app.
  • You can delete saved races inside the app where the feature is available.
  • You can delete your account inside the app where account deletion is available.
  • You can contact support to request help with account or data-related questions.

9. Children

HYROX Lab is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact support.

10. International use

Information may be processed in countries other than your country of residence, depending on the infrastructure used to provide the app.

11. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The effective date at the top of the page indicates the latest version.
